Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a manager?

Click "Register" in the menu and fill out the registration form with your details and team information. Once registered, you can start posting vacancies immediately.

How do players contact me about vacancies?

When players view your vacancies, they can click "Contact Manager" to reach you via email, phone, text message, or WhatsApp.

Is my contact information public?

Yes, when you post a vacancy, your name, email, and phone number are visible to players. This allows them to contact you directly about opportunities. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I delete a vacancy?

Log in, go to "My Vacancies", select your team, and click the "Delete" button next to any vacancy you want to remove.

Can I edit an existing vacancy?

Currently, to update a vacancy, you'll need to delete the old one and create a new one with the updated information.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

Click "Login" in the menu, then click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link.
