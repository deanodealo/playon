Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PlayOn. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform to connect football players with team opportunities.

2. Information We Collect

When you register as a manager on PlayOn, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Team Information: Team name, location
  • Vacancy Information: Position, age group, experience level, and other details about team openings
  • Account Information: Password (encrypted), registration date

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your team vacancies to players searching for opportunities
  • Enable players to contact you regarding vacancies (via email, phone, SMS, or WhatsApp)
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Send important updates about your account or the PlayOn service
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

4. Information Sharing and Display

Public Display: When you post a vacancy, the following information is made publicly visible to all users:

  • Your name (as the manager)
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Team name and location
  • Vacancy details (position, age group, experience level)

This public display is essential to the function of PlayOn - connecting players with teams. By registering and posting vacancies, you explicitly consent to this public display of your contact information.

Third-Party Sharing: We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored using Google Firebase, a secure cloud-based platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the personal information we hold about you
  • Update: Modify your account information at any time through your profile settings
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Remove Vacancies: Delete any or all of your posted vacancies at any time
  • Opt-Out: Stop receiving communications from us (except essential account notifications)

7. Cookies and Tracking

PlayOn uses essential cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking technologies for marketing purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

PlayOn is designed for team managers (adults 18+) to post vacancies for youth football teams. While the platform helps young players find teams, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise any interactions their children have through the platform.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information and vacancies from public display within 30 days, though some information may be retained in backup systems for a limited period as required by law.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using your information is:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for the specific purpose of displaying your contact information to facilitate player-team connections
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests in providing and improving our service

12. International Data Transfers

Your information is stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located in various countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when transferring data internationally.
